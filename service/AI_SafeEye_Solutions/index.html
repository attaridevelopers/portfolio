SafeEye AI Solutions

SafeEye offers AI video analytics and IoT solutions, focusing on enhancing workplace safety. Our safety solutions are deployed across various industries, including Oil & Gas, Construction, Warehousing, Transportation, Manufacturing, Maritime, Industrial sites, and more. Our aim is to empower dedicated safety professionals with extensive safety solutions and behavior-based technologies.

 
 
 

Easy Setup and Application

Only 3 steps to deploy:

  1. Acquire footage using your accessible cameras.
  2. Utilize our AI technology for processing.
  3. Cloud or edge monitoring without the need for additional sensor devices.

At SafeEye, safeguarding data privacy is paramount. Our software never extracts any personally identifiable information from the visual data we analyze.
